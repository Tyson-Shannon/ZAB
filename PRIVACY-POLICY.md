# Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All functionality runs locally in the user’s browser. The extension only modifies Google Search URLs on the device to enable Web-only results using udm=14.

No analytics, tracking, or external network requests are used. No data is sent to any server or third party.

The extension does not access browsing history, form inputs, or any content outside of Google Search navigation events required for its core functionality.
